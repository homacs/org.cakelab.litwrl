Life in the Woods Renaissance

Mod-Pack Update 1.1.5

07/01/2016


The long major update of the mod-pack is finally here. This update brings a lot of updates of mods (including Forge) which contain new content, fixes and improvements to game mechanics, as well as improvements to stability. Below you will find some notes to consider before upgrading, then a brief summary of gameplay related updates and in the end a list of all changes.

Before Upgrading

If you are going to upgrade a world of the previous versions of LitWR (1.0.8 and below), please make a backup first (refer to Section Configuration in the manual to learn how to make backups of your previous version and revert to and keep an old version). When you start the "old" single player world for the first time with the upgraded client, Forge will show you a list of missing blocks of the mod Streams. We have tested a lot and haven't found any issues with missing blocks, so you can accept it and proceed. However, there may be some special cases we haven't tested and this is why you need the backup.

Gameplay Changes

Jute Farming (Vegan Option)

In the previous version jute was a non-regenerative resource, found in certain biomes only and therefore hard to come by. Now jute stalks provide jute seeds which can be planted on farmland!

Even More Cooking Recipes! (Harvest Craft)

Pam does not stop adding new and exiting content for passionate cooks! Besides 29 new cooking recipes she also added more recipes to receive cooking oil from various seeds, nuts and fruits.

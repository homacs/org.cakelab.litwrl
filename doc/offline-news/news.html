[25/06/2016] Mod-pack Update 1.1 Incoming!
On 1st of July we will update the mod-pack to version 1.1.5 which contains a lot of updates for mods, including forge. Today's update of the launcher is a preparation of the coming mod-pack update. Most important: The new launcher allows to keep an older mod-pack version until you decide that it is safe to upgrade to the newer version. We have tested a lot, but there might be some special cases which may cause issues with upgrades of existing saves. Refer to section Configuration in the manual to learn how to keep a given version and check for compatibility of your existing worlds.
